Le Speed Cube aims to connect speedcubers sharing the same passion. We offer individual and group classes for the competitive speedcubers and the casual speedcubers. You will meet people who want to learn the most advanced algorithms and those who simply want to acquire new methodical skills.

Getting into the speedcube world? Try our world class cubes!

Speedcubing (also known as speedsolving) is the hobby involving solving a variety of twisty puzzles, the most famous being the 3x3x3 puzzle or Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible.

Speedcube competitions: Prove your skills!

WCA World Cube Association is the global organizer of speedcube competitions. They organize events all around the world. Each year new World Records (WRs) are broken.
